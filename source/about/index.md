AI SWE with 10 years of experience at <a href="https://blog.google/products/google-cloud/octo-google-clouds-two-way-innovation-street/">Google</a>.


Biography: Lee Boonstra (they/them) is an AI SWE with 10 years of experience at Google, working within the Global Office of the CTO, Innovation Factory. They specialize in building large-scale machine learning systems, distributed training frameworks, and advanced agentic workflows that disrupt markets and foster global collaboration. With over a decade of experience, Lee excels at driving high-velocity innovation projects—collaborating with AI agents in a rapid **Vibe Coding** paradigm—to build state-of-the-art production systems using **LangChain**, **LangGraph**, and **GraphRAG** indexing.

Their technical skills are extensive, spanning Google Cloud, LLM pre-training/fine-tuning, distributed scaling with **vLLM**, as well as Speech Technology and Vector Embeddings. Lee orchestrates enterprise-grade solutions using frontier LLMs including Google's **Gemini**, OpenAI's **GPT**, and Anthropic's **Claude** models. A recent project Lee led involved designing the end-to-end GenAI speech solution for Wendy's, showcasing their ability to build, evaluate, and deploy models to production. Much of their orchestration architecture is powered by Google's internal high-performance **Antigravity Framework**, and Lee's innovations are captured in 3 patent filings (including US20250078820A1) covering transactional agent accuracy and secure cross-boundary agent communication.

Beyond their work as an engineer, Lee is a respected public keynote speaker, having presented at over 50 large conferences, including Google I/O and Google Cloud Next, to audiences of up to 5,000 people. They are also a published author for O'Reilly and Apress, having written the book [The Definitive Guide to Conversational AI with Dialogflow & Google Cloud](https://www.amazon.com/Definitive-Guide-Conversational-Dialogflow-Google/dp/1484270134/ref=asc_df_1484270134/) and recently, Lee authored the viral [Prompt Engineering Whitepaper](https://www.kaggle.com/whitepaper-prompt-engineering) for Google/Kaggle, which has been read by developers, prompt engineers, and AI researchers all over the world.

Lee's journey is a testament to their passion for creating technology rather than just consuming it.
